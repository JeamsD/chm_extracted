Additional resources for APIs and product-specific information are:

SOLIDWORKS

SOLIDWORKS Costing

Start the SOLIDWORKS software, click Help > SOLIDWORKS Help, and open the SOLIDWORKS Costing book.


SOLIDWORKS PDM Professional

Start the SOLIDWORKS PDM Professional Administration tool, log into a SOLIDWORKS PDM vault as admin, expand the vault, right-click Add-ins, and click Programmer's Reference Guide.

Length of fillet on second side
Flag
  • 0 = One-sided and no tangent propagation
  • 1 = Two-sided

EdgeNum1
Number of intersecting edges on first side
DeSelEdge1

Array indicating if intersecting edges are selected (0) or deselected (1) on first side

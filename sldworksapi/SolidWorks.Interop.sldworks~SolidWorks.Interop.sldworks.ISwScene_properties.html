 NameDescription

BackgroundImageGets or sets the background image for this scene.

BackgroundTypeGets or sets the type of background for this scene.

FloorDepthGets or sets the depth of the scene floor.  
FloorDirectionGets or sets whether to flip the direction of the floor of this scene.

FloorReflectionsGets or sets whether to show reflections of the model on the scene floor.  
FloorRotationGets or sets the rotation of the scene floor relative to the environment of this scene.  
FloorSelectionGets or sets the floor appearance of this scene.  
FloorShadowsGets or sets whether to show shadows cast by the model on the scene floor.  
FloorWidthGets or sets the width of the scene floor.  
HorizonHeightGets or sets the height on the high dynamic range imaging (HDRI) scene sphere where the scene floor starts to flatten.  
KeepBackgroundGets or sets whether to retain the background when replacing the scene.  
RenderingBrightnessGets or sets the brightness contributed by the high dynamic range imaging (HDRI) environment.  
SceneReflectivityGets or sets the amount of reflectivity provided by the high dynamic range imaging (HDRI) environment.

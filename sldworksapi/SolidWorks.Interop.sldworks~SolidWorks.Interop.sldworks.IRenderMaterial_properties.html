FileNameGets or sets the path and file name (.p2m) of the appearance.

FitWidthGets or sets whether to stretch the width of the appearance texture to the selected entity when mapping the appearance.  
FixedAspectRatioGets or sets whether the aspect ratio is fixed for mapping this appearance texture.

GlobalIlluminationReceiveGets or sets whether diffuse materials absorb photons for illuminating this appearance.  
GlossyGets or sets the specular factor of the lights reflected by the appearance.  
HeightGets or sets the height for mapping this appearance texture.  
HeightMirrorGets or sets whether to flip the appearance texture about the selected direction vertically.  
IgnoreMissingFileGets or sets whether to ignore any missing image file warnings.  
IlluminationShaderTypeGets or sets the type of illumination of the appearance.  
IndexOfRefractionGets or sets the index of refraction for illuminating this appearance.  
LinkToFileGets or sets whether to link instances of the appearance to an appearance file (.p2m).  
MappingTypeGets or sets the mapping type for this appearance.

ProjectionReferenceGets or sets the projection direction for mapping the appearance.  
ReflectivityGets or sets the reflectivity for illuminating the appearance.  
RotationAngleGets or sets the angle by which to rotate the texture relative to the axes for mapping this appearance.

SecondaryColorGets or sets the secondary color of the appearance.  
SpecularGets or sets the intensity of the light surface for illuminating the appearance.

TertiaryColorGets or sets the tertiary color of the appearance.  
TextureFilenameGets or sets the path and filename of the texture for this appearance.  
ToonShaderTextureFilenameGets or sets the path and filename for the toon shader texture file.  
TranslucencyGets or sets the degree to which the material is able to filter and diffuse light for illuminating the appearance.

WidthGets or sets the width for mapping this appearance texture.  
WidthMirrorGets or sets whether to flip the appearance texture about the selected direction horizontally.  
XPositionGets or sets the X offset direction for the appearance.  
YPositionGets or sets the Y offset direction for the appearance.

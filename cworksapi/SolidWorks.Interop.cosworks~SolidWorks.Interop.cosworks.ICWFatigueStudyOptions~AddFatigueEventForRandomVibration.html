Remarks

This method works only in SOLIDWORKS Simulation Premium.

To fully configure the random vibration fatigue study for this event, call:

  • ICWFatigueStudyOptions::RandomVibrationComputationalMethod
  • ICWFatigueStudyOptions::FatigueStrengthReductionFactor
  • ICWFatigueStudyOptions::ResultFolder
  • ICWFatigueStudyOptions::ShellFace
  • ICWFatigueStudyOptions::SetInfiniteLifeSettings

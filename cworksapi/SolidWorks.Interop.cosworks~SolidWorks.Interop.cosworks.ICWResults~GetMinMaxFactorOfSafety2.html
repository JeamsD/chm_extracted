Remarks

If nonlinearities exist in the study, be advised to not use Factor of Safety to estimate the load-bearing capacity of the system.

For... Specify NComponent with swsFOS_NonCompositeCriterion_e....
Ductile materials
  • swsFOSNonCompositeCriterion_VonMisesHencky

   - or -

  • swsFOSNonCompositeCriterion_Tresca
Brittle materials
  • swsFOSNonCompositeCriterion_MohrCoulomb

   - or

  • swsFOSNonCompositeCriterion_Coulomb
Pure beam studies
  • swsFOSNonCompositeCriterion_Automatic

Remarks

To get the names of the Costing bodies in a Costing part:

  1. Get the Costing bodies in the Costing part by calling:
      • ICostPart::GetBodies 
        - or - 
      • ICostPost::GetBodyCount and ICostPart::IGetBodies.
  1. Get the names of the Costing bodies in the Costing part by iterating through the array of returned Costing bodies and calling ICostBody::GetName.
